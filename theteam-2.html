Our Team

At Uduhu Holdings, our team is the backbone of our success. Our dedicated and passionate professionals bring a wealth of expertise and experience to every project, delivering innovative solutions and unmatched quality to our clients.

Our diverse team of engineers, project managers, and skilled tradespeople work collaboratively to provide exceptional service and exceed expectations. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, integrity, and sustainability in everything we do.

From complex metal fabrication to reliable repair services, our team has the knowledge and expertise to tackle any challenge. We are dedicated to building strong relationships with our clients, ensuring their vision becomes a reality.

Meet the team behind Uduhu Holdings and discover how our commitment to quality and innovation can make your next project a success.

Teboho Mkhwanazi

FOUNDER & CEO

Teboho Mkhwanazi is the Founder and CEO of Uduhu Holdings, bringing over 14 years of experience at Sasol, where he worked as a welding specialist and Quality Controller (QC). A qualified, trade-tested boilermaker, Teboho attended Vaal University of Technology (VUT) and received specialized training from the South African Institute of Welding (SAIW). His extensive expertise in welding and fabrication, coupled with his leadership skills, has been crucial in establishing Uduhu Holdings as a leader in the engineering and construction sectors.

Thato Sete

MANAGING DIRECTOR

Thato Sete is the Managing Director of Uduhu Holdings, with a strong background in civil engineering and project management. He holds a BEng Tech and a National Diploma in Civil Engineering from Tshwane University of Technology and is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP). Thato has extensive experience managing complex infrastructure projects and specializes in using Civil 3D, Prokon, Epanet, MS Project, and AutoCAD. His strategic leadership and commitment to excellence drive the success of Uduhu Holdings.

Reuben Malebana

ENGINEERING MANAGER

Reuben Malebana, with a BEng Tech in Civil Engineering from Tshwane University of Technology (TUT), is a skilled Engineering Manager. He has extensive experience in structural design, analysis, and construction monitoring. Reuben effectively leads engineering teams, delivering innovative solutions and ensuring project success within time and budget constraints.

Reneilwe Pilane

She holds a Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBChB) degree from the University of Cape Town, which highlights her strong analytical and problem-solving skills. Reneilwe has experience in various administrative roles, demonstrating excellence in time management, effective communication, and attention to detail. Her diverse skill set and adaptability make her a valuable asset to Uduhu Holdings, supporting both the administrative and marketing functions.

Sanelisiwe Kheswa

Duze Joseph Mkwanazi

SAFETY OFFICER

Duze Joseph Mkwanazi is a highly skilled and experienced Safety Officer with certifications in Hazard Identification, Risk Management, and SHEQ from IRCA Global, all registered with SACPCMP. With a strong work ethic and attention to detail, he has worked with Bidvest TMS Group and CCI Turnkey Projects, contributing to major projects like the Sasol Shutdown. Fluent in Sesotho, English, IsiZulu, and Afrikaans, Duze excels in managing safety protocols across diverse teams, ensuring compliance and safety in challenging environments.

Frank Makobe

CHIEF SAFETY OFFICER

Frank Makobe is the Chief Safety Officer at Uduhu Holdings, bringing over 34 years of experience from Sasol Group of Companies. He holds a B-Tech in Safety Management and a National Diploma in Safety Management from UNISA. Frank has completed several specialized courses, including Total Quality Management (TQM), Foundation for Management Program (FMP), Management of Others Development Program (MODP), SAMTRAC, NOSA Higher Diploma in Occupational Safety, OSHAS 18001, Construction & Contractor Safety, Legal Liability, Contractor & Construction Management, and accredited auditing techniques. His comprehensive expertise in safety management, risk assessment, and compliance makes him a vital leader in ensuring a safe work environment at Uduhu Holdings.
